GROUP PERSONAL ACCIDENT INSURANCE

    The policy compensates individual against:

  • Accidental Death
  • Permanent Total Disablement
  • Permanent Partial Disablement
  • Temporary Total Disablement
  • Weekly Benefit maximum upto Rs. 5000 upto 100 weeks
  • Worldwide coverage
  • 24 x 7 coverage or can be limited to working hours

    Additional Benefits

  • Medical Expenses :Reimbursement of Medical expenses arising directly and solely out of accident. Reimbursable amount shall be 20% of capital sum insured or 40% of admissible claim amount or actual expenses whichever is lower (payable only when the claim is admissible under basic cover).
  • Expenses for carriage of dead body :-Expenses incurred for carriage of dead body of the insured to the place of residence at 2% of capital sum insured subject to a maximum of Rs. 2500.
  • Children Education fund -10% of capital sum insured per dependent child subject to a maximum of Rs. 5000 up to maximum two dependent children
  • Cremation / Funeral Charges :-The policy reimburses up to 2% of Capital Sum Insured or Rs. 2500/- whichever is less.
  • Modification of residential accommodation and own vehicle :- Permanent Total Disability arising from an accident could sometimes demand a change in lifestyle. In order to meet these requirements Insured might have to invest or modify existing vehicle or invest in making some changes in the house. Reimbursement of 10% of the Principal Sum up to a maximum of Rs.50,000 towards this (payable only when the claim is admissible under basic cover).
  • Ambulance Hiring Charges :-The policy will be reimbursed upto Rs.1,000 for ambulance hiring charges in theevent of an accident.
  • Family Transportation: When as a result of an accident, the principal insured is confined in a hospital outside 100 kms of his residence, we will reimburse the actual transportation expenses incurred by the immediate family member, to reach the insured person up to a maximum of Rs. 25,000

DOCUMENTS FOR CLAIM UNDER GPA POLICY

  • Accidental Death:
    • Claim form duly filled up by the Claimant & signed and stamped by the Insured
    • True copy of FIR (First Information Report), panchnama, any other inquest report
    • True copy of Post Mortem Report
    • Original Death Certificate
    • Letter from the HR as an employee certificate stating details of employee with Date of Joining
    • True copy of Viscera Report if applicable
    • Copy of driving license( verified with original ) if the person was driving
  • Permanent Total Disability:
    • Claim form duly filled up by the insured/Claimant & signed and stamped by the Insured
    • Hospitalization Bills/Papers (indoor case papers)
    • Disability Certificate from a Civil Surgeon/Senior Surgeon from a reputed hospital
    • Letter from the HR as an employee certificate stating details of employee with Date of Joining
    • Police intimation (whether Panchanama or FIR) to know that the case has been reported to the Police
    • Copy of driving license copy ( verified with original ) if the person was driving was driving at the time of accident.
